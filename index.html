Welcome to Zach's Kitchen

We offer catering and private chef services specializing in private parties and meal preparation for families. Our goal is to provide high quality food at a reasonable price to the local community. You can select from our weekly meal menu below or contact me directly for custom orders and event consultations.

Weekly Meal Service Menu

  • Lasagna: Classic lasagna bolognese layered with meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella cheese
  • Chicken & Rice: Cuban-style chicken and rice with marinated chicken and yellow rice tossed with sauteed peppers, onions, garlic and green olives
  • Shepard's Pie: Ground beef and pork sauteed with onions, peas, and carrots, topped with mashed potatoes and a sprinkle of shredded cheddar cheese mixed with bread crumbs
  • Pot Roast: Slow-cooked chuck roast with onions, carrots, and potatoes, served in a brown gravy over white rice
  • Swedish Meatballs: Beef and pork meatballs smothered in a savory brown gravy cream sauce and served with a side of egg noodles
  • Sausage & Peppers: Hot and mild italian sausages roasted over a bed of sliced green/red peppers and onions and tossed in a light marinara sauce, served with a side of pasta

Catering & Event Services
